Group Processes

Week 8:

We met each other and discussed the assignment’s structure as well as studied each other’s strengths and weaknesses. We have also achieved the first stage of getting to know our team members, including the way people talk, how they work and the way to contact each other efficiently.

Then we are to do research about the upcoming topic so that in the next meeting, we can decide which idea we can use in the future.

Week 9:

We have gathered our original ideas as well as taken some great ideas on the internet as a way to compare to our current existing pool of ideas. Then we narrow down each idea until we get the final one

Since our first meeting and our preparation at home were successful,we managed to pin down the main concept of our project to be in servings of clothes suggestions in only this meeting.

Week 10:

We have made a plan of the work during the meeting, then we decide each person’s work, allocate the team’s main focus so as to efficiently work through the process in the minimum time and effort.

Week 11:

Using the Drive and github and Canva and every online tool available, we managed to create a system of writing and checking for errors simutanously in each day: morning we write, and at night we can comment on the changes we have made. This process has been done everyday since the day we have made our plans.

Week 12:

After countless addings and fixings and comments everyday, we have managed to get ourselves a finalised product of the report, a website, a slideshow and finished our preparations for the demonstration day.

Overall:

In the first meeting to get to know each other and discuss the assignment’s structure, we quickly opened up and introduced ourselves as new friends. It was a smooth and comfortable meeting since we were all communicative, active in raising ideas and willing to listen to each other’s opinions and give feedback. Although everyone had different assignments and due dates, we tried to arrange the time as well as planned an agenda and prepared for the assignment before the meeting. During every meeting, we have one person write a meeting minute to record what was discussed and take note of important information as well as the deadline for the group. After dividing tasks, we also supported each other if someone was stuck at anything. For example, when Hung was a little out of ideas for risks, we had sat down and discussed the possibility of risks when doing this app. During the time we worked together, all members have shown respect to each other and put effort into executing the project probably. Overall, it can be said that my group supported each other and worked well to finish this assignment.
